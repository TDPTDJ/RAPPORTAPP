SZV consultation Information This application provides the possibility to generate the information of a provider, institution or medical device. How to Enter the correct values into the form. First you need to enter a valid RIZIV/INAMI number with a maximum of eight numbers. Then you enter the prestation date so you get the information which was valid on that date. Finally, you enter the attribute data. Search RIZIV/INAMI number Attribute class HCW HCI HCA Prestation date
